# Lesson 1 — Foundations of ownership investing  
Invest-ish source documentation

This document lists the educational sources used to inform Lesson 1 content and assessment items.  
Content is paraphrased and adapted for beginner instructional clarity.

---

## Core concepts covered

Lesson 1 introduces the following foundational investing concepts:

- Stocks as ownership
- Alignment between owners and business performance
- Revenue growth as demand signal
- Profit growth as efficiency signal
- Market expectations and pricing
- Expectation surprises and price movement
- Company quality versus investment quality
- Overpaying and return potential
- Beginner evaluation checklists
- Long-term investing perspective
